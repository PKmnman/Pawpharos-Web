How it works.

Pawpharos makes use of Bluetooth Low Energy technology (BLE) alongside a network of sniffer devices placed in your home or business that listen for the signal of a BLE beacon attached to the collar of your pet as they move from room to room. You simply setup your sniffer network, attach the beacon device to your pet's collar, and watch as Pawpharos monitors your pet's location.
